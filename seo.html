Keyword Stuffing

Cheap SEO agencies cram keywords unnaturally into every sentence. Google recognizes this immediately, penalizes your site, and you end up worse off than when you started.

✕

Buying Cheap Links

Link farms and private blog networks look like a shortcut but they're a ticking time bomb. One Google update and your rankings collapse overnight — often taking months to recover.

✕

Ignoring Technical SEO

No amount of content helps if your site is slow, has broken crawl paths, duplicate content, or poor mobile experience. These are the foundations most bad agencies skip entirely.

✕

No Reporting or Transparency

You pay monthly and receive vague updates with no real data. You don't know what's actually being done, what's working, or why your rankings haven't moved in six months.
